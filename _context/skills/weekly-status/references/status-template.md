# Weekly Status Report Template

```markdown
# Week of {Mon date} to {Fri date}: Status Report

**Generated:** {ISO timestamp}
**Sources:** {comma-separated list of systems queried}

---

## Accomplished

### Shipped
- **{JIRA-KEY}**: {title} ([link])
- **PR #{num}**: {title} - merged {date} ([link])
- **{Confluence page}**: {title} - published ([link])

### Progressed
- **{JIRA-KEY}**: {title} - moved from {old status} to {new status}
- **PR #{num}**: {title} - {open/in review}

## Key Decisions
- **{Decision}**: {context in one sentence} (source: {meeting name/Slack channel})

## Meetings ({count} this week)
- Notable: {meeting 1}, {meeting 2}, {meeting 3}

## Blocked / At Risk
- **{JIRA-KEY}**: blocked by {reason} since {date}. Waiting on {person}.

## Carried Forward
- [ ] {Action from weekly-scanner not yet completed}
- [ ] {Action assigned in meeting not yet done}

## Next Week Preview
- {Key meetings from calendar}
- {Upcoming deadlines from Jira}
- {Pending decisions or reviews}

---
*Generated by /weekly-status*
```

## Audience Variants

### Manager/Team (default)
Full template above. One page, scannable in 3 minutes.

### Self (detailed)
Include everything above plus:
- Full list of Jira transitions (not just highlights)
- All PRs with diff stats
- Slack threads participated in
- Personal notes and reflections

### Leadership (executive summary)
Condense to 3-5 bullets:

```markdown
## This Week: {date range}
- {Biggest accomplishment with business impact}
- {Second accomplishment}
- {Key blocker or risk, if any}
- {Decision that needs visibility}
```

## Cadence Guidelines

### Minimum Viable Cadence
- 1 Substack per week (anchor content)
- 2 LinkedIn posts per week
- 3 X posts per week (at least 1 thread)

### Stretch Cadence
- 1 Substack per week
- 3 LinkedIn posts per week
- 5 X posts per week (2 threads)
- 1 TikTok per week

### Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|:-----|:----------|:--------|
| **Deep insight** | 1/week (Substack) | Authority building, SEO, subscriber growth |
| **Professional take** | 2/week (LinkedIn) | Network growth, professional brand |
| **Quick hit** | 3-5/week (X) | Engagement, reach, idea testing |
| **Visual/video** | 1/week (TikTok) | New audience, format experimentation |

## Content Sources

Where to find ideas each week:

1. **Product brain projects**: What's happening in active projects? ("Building in public" angle)
2. **Meeting insights**: Interesting decisions or debates from the week
3. **Industry news**: React to PM/tech news with a contrarian or experienced take
4. **Past experience**: current and previous roles as case studies
5. **Community questions**: What are PMs asking on X/LinkedIn/Reddit?

## Tracking

After publishing, note in the calendar:
- Engagement metrics (likes, comments, reposts) after 48 hours
- Which posts overperformed (reuse that angle/format)
- Which posts underperformed (diagnose: topic, timing, or format issue?)
